## Why

The "Manual Type Override" feature is intended for damage calculations, not for team building. Displaying it in the Team Member editor is confusing and unnecessary for users saving Pokémon to their teams.

## What Changes

- Remove the "Manual Type Override" toggle and type selection inputs from the `TeamMemberEditorModal`.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

### Modified Capabilities
- `team-member-editor`: Remove type override functionality from the editor UI.

## Impact

- **UI**: The Team Member editor will be cleaner and more focused on team-building fields.
