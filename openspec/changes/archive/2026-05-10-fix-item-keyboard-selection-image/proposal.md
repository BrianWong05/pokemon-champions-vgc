## Why
The item search dropdown is missing visual feedback (item images) in the search results, making it inconsistent with the Pokémon search dropdown and harder for users to verify their selection during keyboard navigation.

## What Changes
- Add `ItemImage` to each row in the `ItemSearchSelect` dropdown results.
- Ensure consistent styling with other search components.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `visual-item-search`: Including item images in search results for better visual identification.

## Impact
- Improved UX for item selection.
