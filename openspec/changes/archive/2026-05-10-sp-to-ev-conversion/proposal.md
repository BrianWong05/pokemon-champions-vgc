## Why
Users need a way to convert between Special Points (SP) and Effort Values (EV) within the Damage Calculator interface to streamline team building and stat optimization.

## What Changes
- Add an "SP <-> EV" conversion tool/section to the Pokémon configuration interface.
- Implement conversion logic to calculate EVs from SP and vice versa based on standard Pokémon stat formulas.
