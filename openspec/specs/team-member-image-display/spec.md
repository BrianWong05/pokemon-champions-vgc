# Team Member Image Display

## Purpose
Define the capabilities for visualizing Pokémon sprites on team member cards in the team builder.

## Requirements

### Requirement: Pokemon Image Rendering
The system SHALL display the Pokémon sprite on each member card in the team view.

#### Scenario: Displaying Pokémon image
- **WHEN** the user views their team
- **THEN** each member card displays the correct Pokémon sprite/image.
