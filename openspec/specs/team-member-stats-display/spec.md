# Team Member Stats Display

## Purpose
Define the capabilities for visualizing detailed stat information, including base stats and invested Stat Points (SPs), on team member cards in the team builder.

## Requirements

### Requirement: Stat Visualization on Team Member Cards
The system SHALL display detailed stat information (base stats and SPs) for each Pokémon on the team detail cards.

#### Scenario: Viewing stats on a member card
- **WHEN** the user views the team details
- **THEN** each member card displays the HP, Atk, Def, SpA, SpD, and Spe stats, showing the Base value and SP investment.
